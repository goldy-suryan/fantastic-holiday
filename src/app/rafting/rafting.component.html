RAFTING

PLAYING WITH THE WAVES

Rafting is a sport of adrenaline and the best spot to feel that adrenaline are the rapids near the religious town of Rishikesh. The River Ganga, as it passes through here rages on creating great avenues for rafting. This tour takes you to an amazing adventure camp on the beaches of Rishikesh where you can feel the adrenaline rushing through you as you conquer rapids after rapids
